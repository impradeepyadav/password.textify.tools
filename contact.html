Frequently Asked Support Questions

The generator isn't working in my browser. What should I do?
Try updating your browser to the latest version. We officially support Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. If the problem persists, clear your browser cache or try incognito mode.
Are the passwords really random and secure?
Yes. We use the Web Crypto API, which provides cryptographically secure random numbers. This is the same standard used by banks and governments for secure encryption.
Can I use Password Generator offline?
Absolutely. Once the page loads, you can disconnect from the internet and it still works perfectly. All generation happens locally in your browser.
I lost a password I generated. Can you recover it?
No, we cannot recover any passwords. Since we operate on a zero-knowledge architecture and don't store anything, we have no way to retrieve passwords. Please save your passwords in a password manager immediately after generation.
How do I request a new feature?
Email us at support@passwordgenerator.com with "FEATURE REQUEST" in the subject line. Popular requests include bulk password generation, password strength API, mobile apps, and password history tracking.
How do I report a bug?
Please email us at support@passwordgenerator.com with your browser type and version, operating system, steps to reproduce the issue, and screenshots if applicable.

Report a Security Vulnerability

If you discover a security issue with Password Generator, please follow responsible disclosure:

  1. Email us at security@passwordgenerator.com with full details
  2. Allow us 72 hours to respond and investigate
  3. Don't publicly disclose until we've addressed the issue
  4. We'll credit you in our security acknowledgments (with permission)

We take security seriously and appreciate responsible disclosure.
